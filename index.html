Science in the 21 Century

How the Protocol works

The Science blockchain brings together a combination of technologies and economic incentive systems to guarantee the reproducibility of Science.

Immutable Accounting for Science

A ledger that captures the complete science discovery cycle from ideas to experiment to publishing.

New Science Review

Traditional peer review has taken science a long way forward, new human based review systems will be investigated.

Automated Science Review

Science is producing more data than ever and this is set to exponentially increase as experiments capture data from sensors. Vast quantities of distributed data is the future of science and this opens up the opportunity to find automated ways to design out human bias in experiments and to objectivity 'score' the outcomes of science.

A new currency

The LAB will explore new currencies to sustain the platform, identify reputations and to value the science itself.
